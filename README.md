***Radionics/Orgonomy technology with a modern ADC. 🎯***
+ ***crystal_numbers.txt***

Four small coils connected in series and attached to the microphone jack with a

small container in which the four coils are positioned facing all four cardinal directions, resulting in very high and precise efficiency.

Place your crystal or other iron/metal objects, or whatever type you wish to explore, in the center.

You can also connect piezo elements at the top and bottom for stereo sound!

Alternatively, purchase a USB-C adapter and use your phone as a recording device. Don't forget the coils! (Always record with your phone in airplane mode.)
